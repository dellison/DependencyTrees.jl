Trees

This is where I write about trees.

Dependency structure in natural language consists of directed relations between words in a sentence.

DependencyTrees.DependencyTree — Type.
DependencyTree

A natural language sentence annotated with dependency structure.

Types of tokens

DependencyTrees.UntypedDependency — Type.
UntypedDependency(id, form, head)

Represents a simple untyped (head –> dependent) relation between two words in a sentence.

source
DependencyTrees.TypedDependency — Type.
TypedDependency(id, form, deprel, head)

Represents a typed (head -[deprel]-> dependent) relation between two words in a sentence. id is the index of the word form in the sentence (starting at 1, with 0 meaning the root node). deprel is the dependency relation between this word and its head).

source
